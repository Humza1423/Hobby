Volleyball

I started playing volleyball when I was in grade 3/4. The two main reasons why I started volleyball was because of my brother and an anime that I love. My brother played volleyball in grade 7 and 8. I went to a bunch of his matches and I got inspired. There was also this anime called Haikyuu that played a big part in my journey of volleyball. Haikyuu is based on a short boy who also got inspired from other short volleyball players. However, the main character is special as he jumps very high making up for his height. He always had a disadvantage but he still managed to beat the best of the best. I connected myself with this character because I have the same psychological and physical characteristics as him.
